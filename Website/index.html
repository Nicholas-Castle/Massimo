HISTORY

Massimo Vignelli is one of the world’s most acclaimed graphic designers credited for giving American design aesthetics a modern and streamlined look. His versatility enabled him to work in various areas such as corporate identity, packaging, houseware and furniture, public signage and wayfinding, and environments. Faithful to the Modernist tradition, his designs produced a fruitful career from his native Milan to New York.
